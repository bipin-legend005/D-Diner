Questions for the dossier

Is this experience scary? +
It's suspenseful and dramatic — not horror. There are no jump scares or graphic content. Think Agatha Christie, not a haunted house. All ages 18+ are welcome.
Can strangers team up? +
Absolutely. Solo guests and small groups are seated together and briefed as a team. Our format is designed to spark conversation and collaboration between strangers. Many guests say the new friendships made were the highlight of the evening.
Is food included in the ticket? +
Yes — all tickets include a full 3-course meal (starter, main, dessert) crafted to complement the evening's theme. Beverages are available separately. Vegetarian, vegan, and dietary options available on request at booking.
What should I wear? +
Smart casual to semi-formal. The atmosphere is elegant and noir — think 1920s-inspired looks, cocktail attire, or your sharpest evening wear. Costumes are encouraged but not required. Dress to impress the suspects.
How long does the event last? +
The evening runs approximately 3 hours from start to finish, including the full dining experience, interactive mystery acts, and the grand reveal. Doors open 30 minutes early for arrivals and a welcome drink.
Can I book for corporate groups? +
Yes — we offer private buyouts and custom corporate events for teams of 15–60. Contact us directly for bespoke packages, custom story themes, and branded mystery nights tailored to your company culture.
